# InsightCard

An AI-generated insight callout with a leading icon, a `title`, and body content
passed as `children`. Defaults to the `sparkles` icon; override via `icon` (any
lucide name).

```jsx
<InsightCard icon="trending-up" title="Momentum building">
  Ravi completed 4 story points this week — a 60% jump over last week.
</InsightCard>
```
