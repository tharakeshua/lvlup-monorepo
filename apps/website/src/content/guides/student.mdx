## Introduction

Autograde is paper-first: you sit the exam with pen and paper, your teacher uploads the scanned answer sheets, and the AI grades them against the rubric. Once your teacher reviews and releases the results, you can see how every mark was awarded — question by question, with feedback written for your actual answer.

## Prerequisites

- A student account created by your school admin.
- Your login credentials: email address and password (provided by your admin, or sent to your email on registration).
- The school join code (a short code like `SUB001`) — needed only if your login page asks you to identify your school. Ask your teacher or admin if you are unsure.

---

## Step 1 — Log in to the student portal

Open the student portal link provided by your school. On the login page, enter your **email** and **password**. If prompted for a join code, enter the code your admin shared.

Once logged in, you land on your **Dashboard** — the home page showing your enrolled spaces, upcoming activity, and notifications.

---

## Step 2 — Find your exams

Your exams are set up by your teacher before the exam date. You do not need to do anything in the portal before sitting the exam — just know that the exam exists and attend on the day.

If you want to check which exams are scheduled, look at your **Dashboard** for any exam-related announcements, or check **Notifications** (the bell icon) for messages from your teacher.

---

## Step 3 — Sit the exam on paper

On the exam day, you write your answers on physical paper as usual. No device is needed during the exam itself. Your teacher collects the answer sheets and uploads them to the platform after the exam.

---

## Step 4 — Wait for results — they are sealed until released

After the exam, your teacher uploads and grades the answer sheets. During this time, if you open an exam result link, you will see the result is **sealed** — the score and feedback are withheld until your teacher explicitly releases them.

You will receive a **notification** (visible in the Notifications page, and optionally via email) as soon as your teacher releases results. Until that notification arrives, there is nothing you need to do.

---

## Step 5 — View your released results

When your results are released, open the notification or navigate to **My Progress** from the sidebar.

### My Progress — the Exams tab

**My Progress** (`/results`) is your personal results hub. It shows three tabs:

- **Overall** — a summary of your performance across all exams and spaces
- **Exams** — a list of every exam you have results for, with scores and letter grades
- **Spaces** — progress through your study spaces

Click the **Exams** tab and select the exam you want to review. This opens the **Exam Result** page.

### Exam Result page

The Exam Result page shows:
- **Total score** and **letter grade** (see the grade scale below)
- A **question-by-question breakdown** — each question card shows:
  - Your score for that question (for example, 7 / 10)
  - A green check mark if you answered fully correctly, a red cross if not, or a dash for partial credit
  - **AI feedback** — a written explanation of what you did well, what was missing, and what a stronger answer would include
  - Strengths and areas for improvement listed separately

Use this feedback to understand exactly where marks were lost — it is specific to your answer, not generic.

---

## Step 6 — Track your progress over time

Return to **My Progress → Exams** after each result is released to see how your scores trend across different subjects and exams. The **Overall** tab provides a summary view across all your activity on the platform.

---

## Grade scale

All exam scores are mapped to the following **8-letter canonical scale**:

| Letter | Minimum % |
|--------|-----------|
| A+     | 90        |
| A      | 80        |
| B+     | 70        |
| B      | 60        |
| C+     | 50        |
| C      | 40        |
| D      | 33        |
| F      | 0         |

If you score 65 out of 100, for example, you receive a **B**. If you score 50 out of 80 (62.5%), you also receive a **B**.

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

**My result shows "sealed" — when will it be released?**
Only your teacher can release results. There is no set time — it depends on how long grading and review takes. You will receive a notification the moment your teacher releases them.

**I can see the exam in the portal but there are no results yet.**
This is normal. Results only appear after your answer sheets have been uploaded, graded, reviewed, and released by your teacher. If a long time has passed, check with your teacher.

**I do not agree with my score on a question.**
The AI score is reviewed by your teacher before release. If you believe a score is wrong, speak to your teacher directly — they can check the override history and correct any errors before (or after) release if your school's policy allows it.

**I cannot log in.**
Check your email address is correct and try resetting your password from the login page. If the reset email does not arrive, ask your admin to resend your credentials from the Users page.

**Where is the "My Progress" page?**
Look for the **My Progress** link in the left sidebar or navigation menu. It points to `/results` in the student portal.
