## Introduction

As a parent, you see your child's exam results after the teacher reviews and releases them — never before. You get the total score, the letter grade, and a question-by-question breakdown with the AI-written feedback that explains where marks were awarded or lost.

## Prerequisites

- A parent account linked to your child's school account. Your account is created by the school admin — you will receive login credentials by email.
- The parent portal URL, provided by your child's school.

---

## Step 1 — Log in to the parent portal

Open the parent portal link your school provided. Enter your **email** and **password** to log in.

If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot password** link on the login page. The reset email goes to the address your school registered for you. If you cannot access that email, contact your school admin to update your account.

---

## Step 2 — View your linked children

After logging in, the **Dashboard** shows your linked child (or children). If you have more than one child enrolled at the same school, each appears as a separate card.

Click a child's name or card to see their exam results.

---

## Step 3 — Wait for results to be released

Results are **sealed** until the teacher explicitly releases them. This is by design — your child's teacher reviews every AI-scored answer before making results visible.

You will receive a **notification** (shown in the Notifications page inside the portal, and optionally by email) when results are released. Until then, sealed exams are listed but show no score or feedback.

---

## Step 4 — View a released exam result

Open the notification or navigate to **Exam Results** from the dashboard. Click on the released exam.

The result page shows:
- **Total score** — marks earned out of the total available
- **Letter grade** — the canonical grade band for the percentage score (see the grade scale below)
- **Percentage** — calculated automatically from total score / total marks

---

## Step 5 — Read the per-question breakdown

Scroll down past the summary to the **Per-Question Breakdown** section. Each question shows:
- The question number
- Your child's score for that question (for example, 6 / 10)
- **AI-generated feedback** — a written explanation of what was answered correctly, what was missing, and what a stronger answer would have included
- Strengths and areas for improvement, listed separately

This feedback is specific to your child's actual answer — not a generic comment — so it tells you precisely where they need to focus their revision.

---

## Step 6 — Download the PDF report

On the exam result page, click the **Download PDF** button. The report includes the summary and the per-question breakdown in a formatted document you can save, print, or share with a tutor.

---

## Grade scale

All exam scores are mapped to the following **8-letter canonical scale**:

| Letter | Minimum % |
|--------|-----------|
| A+     | 90        |
| A      | 80        |
| B+     | 70        |
| B      | 60        |
| C+     | 50        |
| C      | 40        |
| D      | 33        |
| F      | 0         |

A score of 75 out of 100 (75%) maps to **B+**. A score of 50 out of 80 (62.5%) maps to **B**. A score of 32 out of 100 (32%) maps to **F**.

## FAQ & Troubleshooting

**The exam shows "sealed" — when will it be released?**
Only the teacher controls when results are released. There is no fixed timeline — it depends on how long grading and teacher review takes. You will receive a notification as soon as the teacher releases the results.

**I cannot see my child in the portal.**
Your parent account must be linked to your child's student account by the school admin. Contact the admin and ask them to link your account to your child's record from the Users → Parents tab.

**I did not receive the notification email.**
Check your spam folder. If the email is not there, log in to the portal and check the **Notifications** page — in-portal notifications are always delivered even if email delivery fails.

**Can I see results before the teacher releases them?**
No. This is intentional — the teacher reviews AI scores before they become visible to ensure accuracy. The platform does not allow parents to bypass the release gate.

**The PDF download did not work.**
Try refreshing the exam result page and clicking Download PDF again. PDF generation happens on demand and may take a few seconds on the first request. If the button remains unresponsive, try a different browser.

**My child attends two schools using LvlUp Autograde — will I see both?**
Each school is a separate tenant with its own parent portal URL. You will need a separate parent account per school. Contact each school's admin for access.
