AutoGrade -- Intelligence
AutoLevelUp
4 Ways to Grade
Criteria-Based
Grade each question against specific marking criteria you define. Best for structured exams.
Dimension-Based
Evaluates answers across four key areas: accuracy, completeness, structure, and clarity. Best for essays and case studies.
Holistic
A single, overall assessment of answer quality. Best for creative writing and open-ended work.
Hybrid
Combines specific criteria with overall quality assessment. Best for exams that need both detail and big-picture evaluation.
What Every Student Receives After an Exam
Every graded answer comes with:
  1. 1Score -- their mark with a clear breakdown of how it was earned
  2. 2Strengths -- what they did well
  3. 3Next Steps -- specific, actionable advice for doing better
  4. 4Key Takeaway -- the one thing to remember from this question
  5. 5Mistake Pattern -- what kind of error was made, so they learn to avoid it
  6. 6Review Flag -- answers the AI is less sure about are sent to the teacher
AI grades. Teachers decide.
Your teachers can override any grade with one click. Every change is documented. Teachers always have the final word.
